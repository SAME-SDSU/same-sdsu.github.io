Welcome to SAME@SDSU!

Official website of the Society of American Military Engineers (SAME) student chapter at San Diego State University (SDSU).

What is SAME?

The Society of American Military Engineers (SAME) unites public and private sector individuals and organizations from across the architecture, engineering, construction, environmental, facility management, contracting and acquisition fields and related disciplines in support of the United States' national security.

SAME San Diego Post →

What is SAME@SDSU?

We are the official student chapter to connect SDSU undergraduates with the military engineering industry by offering mentoring, internships, scholarships, and social activities that foster professional relationships and enhance future career opportunities.

SAME@SDSU was established in October 2011.
